An OTR is using the transtheoretical model of change to guide intervention planning for a client with substance use disorder who voluntarily entered a residential rehabilitation

Scenario item 1 of 4
Which of the following actions should the OTR take when using the principles of motivational interviewing to engage the client in the evaluation process?
You need to select 3 choices
O Offer the client educational information on the benefits of seeking treatment and making lifestyle changes
Express empathy by letting the client know that change is difficult.
Address argumentative behaviors by posing reflective questions.
O Provide immediate feedback to correct negative feelings and thoughts expressed by the client.
O Give the client a list of structured and meaningful activities to promote recovery.
