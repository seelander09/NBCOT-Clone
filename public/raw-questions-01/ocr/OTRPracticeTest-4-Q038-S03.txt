An OTR is working with a client in the home health setting who has Stage IV amyotrophic lateral sclerosis. The spouse, who is the primary caregiver, tells the OTR that the client now
requires more assistance for ADL and recently transitioned to using a manual wheelchair for mobility.
Scenario item 3 of 4
The OTR determines the client has Fair minus (3-/5) functional muscle strength in both upper extremities and notes the client's recent increase in coughing when eating and difficulty
holding built-up utensils at mealtime. What action should the OTR take to enable the client to maintain independence during meals?
You need to select 3 choices
O Provide a swivel spoon to support independence at mealtime
O Refer for further evaluation of a feeding tube.
O Recommend the family feed the client during mealtime.
